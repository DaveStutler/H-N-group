Introduction

At H&N Group, we provide professional drafting services tailored to homeowners and developers across California. We specialize in detached and attached Accessory Dwelling Units (ADUs), helping our clients maximize their property’s potential.

Our team focuses on accuracy, building code compliance, and clear design to ensure your plans move smoothly through city approval. Whether you’re adding a backyard unit or converting existing space, we’re here to turn your vision into reality.
